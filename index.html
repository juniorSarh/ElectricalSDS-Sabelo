OSHA Electrical Safety SDS

Based on OSHA Standard 1926.431

Key Hazards

  • Electrical Shock
  • Fire Risk
  • Tripping Hazard
  • Jewellery Conduction Risk

OSHA 1926.431 outlines safety requirements for electrical installations, use of equipment, insulation standards, and handling procedures. All equipment must be properly maintained and free of recognized hazards likely to cause death or serious physical harm.
